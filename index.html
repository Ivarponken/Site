About me

Hi, I'm Ivar—professional button pusher and part-time keyboard wizard. I spend my days smashing keys, hoping code compiles, and pretending I understand why it doesn’t. I'm basically the digital equivalent of duct tape: a little messy, but somehow everything stays together. When I'm not busy trying to convince JavaScript to behave, I’m probably over-caffeinating, Googling "how to fix broken code," or discovering creative new ways to break websites.
